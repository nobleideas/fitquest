# 🏋️ FitQuest

> **Train your body, not your brain.**

FitQuest is a cross-platform social fitness platform built with Flutter that helps lifters spend less time planning workouts and more time training.

Unlike traditional workout trackers that only record completed workouts, FitQuest actively helps users decide what to do next through intelligent workout rotation, progressive overload tracking, and social sharing.

🌐 **Website:** https://fitquest.space

---

# Why I Built FitQuest

I wanted a workout app that focused on one simple goal:

**Help me make progress without spending mental energy planning every workout.**

Most workout apps are great at logging what you've already done.

FitQuest goes a step further by helping users:

* Track progressive overload
* Automatically rotate exercises
* Build balanced workouts
* Share exercises and equipment with friends
* Stay focused on training instead of planning

---

# Features

### 🔐 Authentication

* Secure account creation and login
* User profiles

### 🏋️ Workout Tracking

* Log sets and repetitions
* Track total training volume
* Review previous workout history

### 🎲 Intelligent Workout Rotation

* Generate balanced Push, Pull, or Legs/Core workouts
* Rotate exercises automatically
* Randomize individual exercises
* Reduce decision fatigue while maintaining variety

### 👥 Social Features

* Add friends
* Share exercise libraries
* Share equipment libraries
* Import shared content directly into your account

### 🎥 Exercise Form Videos

* Upload form videos
* Review technique
* Keep training resources connected to each exercise
